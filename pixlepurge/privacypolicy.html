Last Updated: 31 Jan 2025

1. Information We Collect

Pixel Purge does not collect, store, or share any personal data. However, the app may request access to:

2. How We Use Information

We use the requested permissions only for:

We do not store or transmit any user data to external servers. All image processing happens locally on the device.

4. Third-Party Services

Pixel Purge does not integrate with third-party analytics or advertising services that collect personal data.

5. User Control & Permissions

Users can grant or revoke storage permissions at any time through their device settings. The app remains functional within the granted permissions.
